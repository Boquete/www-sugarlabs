Getting Started

Introdution
This is what you see when you enter Sugar- home screen. In the middle you can notice your icon which you choosed in first OS start. Around your icon there are activities, you can download more activities by entering browser and visiting activities.sugarlabs.org

Changing views
By pressing on keyboard f1 - f6 you easy and fast change between views.

f1 - Neighborhood
f2 - Group
f3 - Home
f4 - Journal
f5 - Activity
f6 - Frame

Neighborhood
Find other Sugar users, make friends, invite to your activities!

Group
You will find here friends found in neighborhood! Invite them to your activities, make groups.

Home
You can find here activities, click on your icon to logout/turn off or enter your user setting! Use browser to download new activities.

Journal
In Journal you can see last activities, browser history, downloaded files and everything you made, check detailed information!

Activity
Want to back to last activity? Just press f5 to change view!

Frame
Frame is organizated by categories:
TOP
Actions & places
RIGHT
People
BOTTOM
Devices
LEFT
Objects in clipboard

Want to share activities?
1) Open activity
2) Click activity icon
3) Click circle icon with dot/dots
4) Choose sharing in My Neighbourhood

Invite friends
Go to Group view and invite your friends!

Frame
1) Views (neighbourhood, group, home, activity)
2) Journal & Activities
3) User
4) Connection, speech, volume, battery

Want to connect AP?
In Neighbourhood view you can see icons:

1) Acces Point with example 20% signal strength
2) Acces Point with example 80% signal strength
3) Acces Point connected with example 20% signal strength
4) Acces Point connected with example 80% signal strength
In the corner you can also see: star (favourite), lock (secured) networks.

One core Sugar
When you move your mouse over a icon you can see that menu appear!
